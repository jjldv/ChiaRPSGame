How to Play

1. Connect

Log in using your Goby Wallet to start playing

2. Create or Join

Open a new game with your bet or join an existing one

Make your move and win XCH rewards

Game Rules

  • Choose your move and secure it with a private key
  • Players have 24 hours to reveal their moves
  • If Player 1 doesn't reveal, Player 2 can claim the total bet
  • Games without opponents can be closed after 24 hours
